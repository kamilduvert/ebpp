About Us

The East Bali Poverty Project (EBPP) is a non-profit organisation helping the thousands of people living in abject poverty without water, sanitation, roads, schools, health facilities and electricity in East Bali.

Our Mission is to eliminate poverty and promote culturally sensitive, sustainable social and economic development in the impoverished rural communities of East Bali, Indonesia, prioritising the health, nutrition and education of children.

Help us to help them help themselves.

Programs

Nutrition Improvement

We educate mothers about nutrition, give nutritional supplements to 1,500 infants and introduce kitchen gardens to malnourished children’s families.

Educational Initiatives

We believe in education as the key for sustainable development and through our 6 hamlet schools provides impoverished children with education and life skills.

Health Awareness and Outreach

We provide comprehensive programmes to 12,000 families, which includes maternal and child health, dental care, basic hygiene and sanitation, and first aid training

Water, Sanitation, Infrastructure

We provide access to first clean water, toilets and road infrastructure to improve the livelihoods of the approx. 3,500 families living in 19 remote East Bali hamlets

Sustainable Agriculture

We teach children and their families to grow organic vegetable gardens for a balanced diet and a sustainable, self-reliant future

Bamboo, Environment and Economy

We organize Vetiver grass, bamboo and agroforestry initiatives drive sustainable community development. Visit our social entreprise: East Bali Bamboo Bikes !
